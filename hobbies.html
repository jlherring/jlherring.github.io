Hobbies, Hobbies, Everywhere!

Squeaking out projects in the little free time I do have

  • Updated December 2022

Whether I'm on or off the clock, I love learning new skills and getting creative. My husband, Kyle, and I enjoy working on projects together. Since the arrival of our baby girl, Addy, in 2021, we value our time together as a family even more. We look forward to introducing her to all of our hobbies and can't wait to see what she enjoys!

Sewing, Quilting, and General Crafting

My grandmother was a masterful quilt maker. I inherited her love of fabrics and sewing, and upon her passing, also inherited her sewing machines. From a practical perspective, I've learned to create what I need around the house, including custom curtains and pillows for Addy's room. Alongside the wreath for her door listed in More Projects, she enjoys a set of custom felt baby milestone numbers and letters. Below, left, she grabs the "2" from her 12-month photo shoot. Even before Addy's arrival, I created felt bouquets for myself and the bridal party for my wedding in 2018. Below, right, my sister and matron-of-honor shows off our felt flowers in front of the main dive tank at the North Carolina Aquarium at Fort Fisher. Finally, when sis tells you that her son loves space and dinosaurs, you have to make Space Dinosaur blanket (below, right)?
